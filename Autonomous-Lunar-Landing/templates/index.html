ABOUT THE PROJECT

🚀 Lunar Lander AI (Reinforcement Learning)

🧠

Proximal Policy Optimization

This project uses the PPO reinforcement learning algorithm, allowing the agent to learn stable landing strategies through continuous interaction with the environment.

🌙

LunarLander-v3 Environment

The spacecraft must land safely between two flags while controlling its speed, angle, and fuel usage under realistic physics simulation.

📈

Learning Through Rewards

Instead of following hard-coded rules, the AI improves by receiving rewards for successful actions and penalties for unsafe behavior.

🎯

Final Goal

Achieve smooth, stable, and accurate autonomous landings with an average reward above the Gymnasium success threshold.
